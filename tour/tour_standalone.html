Your rates — changes apply to your widget immediately.

The Pricing Wizard — your secret weapon
Not sure what your per-linear-foot rates are? No problem. The wizard asks you to price a standard 4’ run of cabinets in a few common variations — just enter what you’d actually charge a customer for each one. It reverse-engineers your rates automatically from your real-world pricing. There’s also a Mini Wizard on any individual line item you’re unsure about — same idea, one item at a time.
🧙 Pricing Wizard — Step 1 of 3
Quote a standard 4-foot run of cabinets
We’ll ask you to price the same 4’ run a few different ways — different materials, with and without doors, supply only vs. installed. Just enter what you’d actually charge a customer for each scenario. The wizard calculates your per-linear-foot rates automatically.
Scenario 1 of 4: 4 ft uppers + 4 ft bases — Melamine box, no doors, supply only (no install). What would you charge?
💡 Mini Wizard: Already have most rates set but unsure about one item? Click "Edit shop items" and use the mini wizard on just that line — enter your price for a single 4’ run scenario and it calculates that rate on its own.
